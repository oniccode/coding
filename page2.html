About The Restaurant

McDonald's is the largest fast food restaurant chain in the world. It's well known for its hamburgers, french fries, and name-brand sandwiches such as the Big Mac, Quarter Pounder, and Egg McMuffin, as well as the child-focused Happy. They Have their very own McDonald's app where they give deals on their food.
